Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Linquo.

How do I get started with Linquo?

Just copy and paste one line of code into your website’s <head> section. Your free chat widget goes live instantly.

Is Linquo really free forever?

Yes. Linquo is completely free — no trials, no credit card, and no hidden limits. You can use it as long as you want.

Can I use Linquo with my team?

Absolutely. Invite teammates to join your workspace, share chats, and collaborate on customer support at no cost.

What types of businesses can use Linquo?

Linquo works for everyone — from startups and SaaS products to e-commerce shops and agencies. If you want to talk to your customers, Linquo is for you.

Does Linquo work on any website platform?

Yes. Linquo integrates easily with Webflow, Framer, Next.js, WordPress, or any custom site with a simple script.

Are there any limits on chats or users?

No. You get unlimited chats, unlimited history, and unlimited teammates — all free.

Is Linquo secure?

Yes. All conversations are encrypted and stored safely. Your customer data is protected with enterprise-grade security.

Can I customize the chat widget?

Yes. You can change colors, position, and branding so the widget matches your website’s look and feel.
