About Linquo

We believe customer support should be simple, instant, and free.

A Team Dedicated to Simplicity & Support

Linquo was created with one clear mission: to make customer communication effortless.We're a team of builders who believe every business, from startups to global brands, deserves free, reliable live chat.

Trusted by 3K+ companies around the world

Guided by Purpose and Principles

At Linquo, our values shape everything we build. Customer support isn't just a feature, it's a human connection.

People First

Our users and their customers always come first. When conversations flow and people feel supported, businesses thrive.

Lead with Empathy

Every chat matters. We listen, we care, and we design with empathy — because real support means understanding before responding.

Grow Together

Linquo grows with our community. We believe progress happens when businesses, teams, and customers lift each other up.
